"I generally shoot black and white with old mechanical cameras. I like how film photography slow down the way I observe and engage with my surroundings. Through the development and printing stages, I am able to revive and enhance the connection I had with the subjects I captured in the shot, and each picture becomes a more vivid and long-lasting experience into my memory."
